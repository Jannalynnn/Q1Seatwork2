Personality and Characteristics
Maria Clara was a nice and kind young lady. Maria Clara, who had been raised with tenderness, was well-loved by everyone around her and might be inspired to acts of generosity, as in the case of the leper. Although this, Maria Clara was split between her love for Ibarra and her devotion for her family, finally opting to safeguard Capitan Tiago's reputation, despite regretting the impact her decision had on Ibarra.
