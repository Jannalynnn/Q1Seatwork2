Character Analysis
He is a morally decent man of Spanish origin who admires both Crisóstomo Ibarra and the late Don Rafael. He is also a Civil Guard lieutenant. He is one of the few who openly supports the Ibarras and expresses his displeasure with Father Dámaso's rule. He tells Crisóstomo Ibarra about his father's fate and how Father Damaso was engaged in his death.
