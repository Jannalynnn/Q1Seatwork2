Character Analysis
Father Sibyla, a priest working in Manila's Binondo area, is a sensible and calm counterbalance to the normally mainly corrupt Father Dámaso and the bizarre Father Salvi. Father Sibyla is a skilled and astute orator who clearly enjoys antagonizing the pretentious Father Dámaso at Ibarra's return celebration.
