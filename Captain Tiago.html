Character Analysis
Capitan Tiago is unique in that he is a rich native-born Filipino. Despite having little respect for religion, he maintains tight relationships with high-ranking members of the Catholic Church and brazenly joins others in racial remarks directed against his own people. His main preoccupation is marrying off his daughter, Mara Clara, to a rich guy from a powerful family. This is one of the key reasons he is eager to abandon his loyalty to Ibarra when branded a dissident. His preference for beneficial social pairings prompts him to accept Linares as a prospective new mate for his daughter.
